About Us

Welcome to Phantom 6901, a high school robotics team located in Corpus Christi, Texas. Our mission is to collaborate, innovate, and achieve new heights in robotics while learning and growing together.

Our Robot: Daniella

Slides Mechanism

Daniella uses Misumi slides to reach high baskets efficiently during matches allowing us to score a high amount of points.

Arm and Claw

The arm, attached to two axons, rotates into the submersible providing near a foot of reach, which a claw attatched at the end collects a sample to be delivered to the highbasket.

Iterations

Our biggest iteralation was adding a climb system, we have also changed the angle of the slides which eliminates the need of a return string. As of right now we are tuning our robot to effortlessly score specimens.
